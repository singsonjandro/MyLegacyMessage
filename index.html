Our Story

My Legacy Message was founded by Mark, Ryan, and Luke after each experienced the profound loss of a loved one. Inspired by the desire to preserve heartfelt words and memories, they created a service that securely stores your personalised video messages and delivers them to your loved ones — often as a touching surprise — after you’ve passed.
