How It Works

  1. Record Your Video(s) – Create the videos you want to share.
  2. Send to Us – Upload or send your videos securely.
  3. Secure Storage – Videos are safely stored online and as a hard copy.
  4. Three-Phase Annual Check-In
    • Email: Each year, we send you a check-in email.
    • Phone: If there's no response within 1 week, we call the number you've provided.
    • Friend/Family Contact: If there's still no response after another week, we contact the friend or family member you've nominated.
    • Outcome: Your nominated contact will confirm whether you are unreachable (e.g., overseas) or have unfortunately passed. At that point, all your videos will be released to the people you've designated to receive them.
