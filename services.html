Three-Phase Annual Check-In

Email:

Each year, you’ll receive a check-in email.

Phone:

If no response within 1 week, we’ll follow up with a phone call.

Friend/Family Contact:

If still no response, we’ll contact your nominated family or friend.

Outcome:

Your contact will confirm if you are unreachable or have passed on, and we will deliver your videos.
