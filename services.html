How it works?

Record your Video(s):

Create the videos you want to share.

Send it to Us:

Upload or send your videos securely.

Secure Storage:

Videos are safely stored online and as a hard copy.

Three-Phase Annual Check-In

Email:

Each year, you’ll receive a check-in email.

Phone:

If no response within 1 week, we’ll follow up with a phone call to the number you’ve provided.

Friend/Family Contact:

If still no response, we’ll contact your nominated friend or family member.

Outcome:

Your friend or family will either confirm you are unreachable (e.g. overseas) or that you have unfortunately passed on. At that point, we will send your video/s to everyone you have nominated to receive them.
